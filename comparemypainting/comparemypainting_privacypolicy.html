Dunally Software - Compare My Painting - Privacy Policy

Introduction

Compare My Painting is a utility app for those learning to paint or draw, to simplify comparing your work to the subject. Dunally Software has developed this Privacy Policy to explain how we collect, use, disclose and transfer your information. By using the application, you consent to the privacy practices described here; please take a moment to familiarise yourself with these practices.

Data Storage - Dunally Software

Dunally Software does not have access to any of your data. The app does not communicate with any Dunally Software servers.

Camera

Compare My Painting allows you to take photographs of your subject and/or painting for visual comparison by the user. The images are only stored and used directly on your device, and not sent to any servers.

Advertising

Future versions of Compare My Painting may include third-party advertisements. These advertisements will come from Google AdMob, and will be subject to Google's privacy policy, which you can see here.
