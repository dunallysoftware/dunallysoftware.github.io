Enhance your Spanish vocabulary with our word search game

Are you learning Spanish? Palabras Ocultas (Hidden Words) generates grids of Spanish words for you to find. As well as spelling, words are spoken as you find them (or you can hear the Spanish to help you find the word). You also have the option to try to speak a Spanish word and have the game find it from your pronunciation.

This Spanish learning app is designed to let you learn new words any time you have a few minutes to play.
